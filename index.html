Serverless Route Optimization

Serverless architecture for identifying the optimal route from a Kinesis stream of geocoordinates. A difficult challenge products providing routing services face would be identifying the optimal route given points of interest. There are various factors that influence the selection of the optimal route (weather, traffic, nearby events etc).

This project focuses on identifying the optimal route from a stream of points of interest that minimizes the total great-circle distance covered.
